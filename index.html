What is Drive App?

The project is to design the UI/UX for a new taxi app that allows users to easily request and pay for rides and provide a convenient experience to drivers and complete ride requests.

Project Goal

The goal of an app project is to create a product that provides value to its users and meets the objectives of the client organization.

Digital Drive Solution Company

The aim is to user convenience and safety through a digital solution. The app has a request screen for selecting pickup and drop-off locations and viewing pricing.

Working on the Style

The main part of the project was to create a style for the app. We worked to create a simple and user-friendly style with a yellow and black theme. We provided a few variations of the style.

Process of Work

When the style was Approved we proceed with the rest screens.

Splash Screens

Meanwhile, we'd been working on splash screen illustrations. Started from the idea and simple sketches and created proper illustrations presenting the idea of the app.

Choose a Vehicle

Users can select their preferred car type based on their needs and budget. Once they have selected their car type, they can proceed with their request and wait for a driver to accept the request.

Convenience Ride Service

Focused on creating a simple and intuitive interface that provides ease and convenience for both riders and drivers. We aimed for a clean and intuitive design that makes the app easy to use.

Summary

Had a great experience working with UI The Company Digital Drive Solution company. The transportation system has improved efficiency and optimized using Drive App Service.

Color

We've been attentive with colors and tried to be as simple as possible to create a clear and user-friendly interface.
